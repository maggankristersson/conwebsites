Billiard Champions of Saudi Arabia

Meet the Billiard brothers, two of the most talented billiard players in Saudi Arabia. Al and Lunkan have won multiple national and international championships over the years.

About the Billiard Brothers

Al and Lunkan are brothers from Riyadh, Saudi Arabia who started playing billiards at a young age. They quickly developed a natural talent for the game and began competing in local tournaments.

As they got older, the brothers started to train more seriously and participate in national and international championships. They soon became the top billiard players in Saudi Arabia, winning numerous titles and setting new records.

As of now, they have produced multiple TV-series about the success which is funded by the Saudi prince Mohammed Bin Salman. Estimates show that the production costed around 9,7 billion dollars for the first season, where the brothers them self recieved about 80% in salary.
