About Wackypedia

Are you tired of boring, stuffy encyclopedias? Look no further! Wackypedia is the internet's ultimate source for questionable information.

Our team of highly unqualified editors and contributors work tirelessly to bring you the most unreliable, inaccurate, and downright hilarious content on the web.

From conspiracy theories to celebrity gossip, we've got it all. And if we don't, just go ahead and make it up! That's the beauty of Wackypedia.
